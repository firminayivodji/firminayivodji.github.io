  1. Cross-sectional Skewness with Jessica A. Wachter

    2. This version: June 2021 | Review of Asset Pricing Studies, forthcoming.

    We evaluate the skewness in the cross-section of returns in light of predictions from a well-known class of models.

Working Papers

  1. High-Frequency Expectations from Asset Prices: A Machine Learning Approach with Aditya Chaudhry

    2. This version: September 2020.

    We construct a daily measure of GDP expectations that can be used to test many empirical anomalies observed in the asset market.

    Presentations: Chicago Econ, Chicago Booth, 2020 Bergen FinTech Conference, Bank of England, SoFiE Machine Learning Conference, SoFiE Annual Meeting

    Awards: 2020 Arnold Zellner Doctoral Prize


  2. Measuring Institutional Pressure for Greenness: A Demand System Approach with Don Noh

    3. This version: May 2021.

    We quantify the institutional price pressure induced by demand for green stocks.

    Presentations: MFR Summer Session 2020, Chicago Booth, CAFM 2020, AFA PhD Poster 2021

    Awards: 2020 CAFM Best PhD Paper Award


  3. Social Inflation

    4. This version: June 2021.

    I uncover a new aggregate risk for the insurance sector stemming from social factors such as large jury awards and broader definitions of liability.

    Presentations: IRMC 2020, EGSC 2020, CAFM 2020, AFA PhD Poster 2021, EFA 2021, WFA 2021

    Awards: WFA PhD Candidate Award For Outstanding Research, 2nd Place in 3MT Competition
