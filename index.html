Welcome to my website! I am a PhD Candidate in Economics and Data Science at the University of Montreal.


My research interests include Econometrics, Big Data, Machine Learning/Text as Data (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), Generative AI, and Climate Finance. I am also interested in applying Artificial Intelligence methods to answer macro-finance and causal inference questions.


In August 2024, I will visit the University of Chicago, Booth School of Business as a Young Scholar.


I hold an MSc. in Statistics and Economics, and have worked as an economist at the Bank of Canada, IMF, and World Bank, as well as an AI Scientist in the IT Industry. I have been honored as a Rising Star in Management Science & Engineering and also recognized as a Becker Friedman Institute Macro-Finance Research Young Scholar.


Please see my CV for more details.
